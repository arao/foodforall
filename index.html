About Us

The idea to serve free Food just outside the AIIMS campus to the needy people came in the mind when member of AIIMS staff while moving through AIIMS entry gate in the month of January 2017 . Immediately they shared this with his ICU collegeous. Soon the idea was implemented with the support of all youngsters.

The first event of free Food event was organised on 5th January on the Birthday Anniversary of Shree Guru Gobind Singh ji named “ Food For All “ Today with the support of hundreds of collegeous from AIIMS and Outside the AIIMS we are successfully organising the Free Food camps twice in a month just outside the AIIMS near the Metro gate no. 2. Till october last we have organised 18 events by distributing quality food to thousands of needy people On the occasion of 15th event we had registered our Food for All group as a NGO ( registration no. 2142 ).

Food For All is started with two members and now it has 150+ members till october 2017.

Objectives

  1. Serve food outside the hospital for patient and their attendants.
  2. Provide education/ useful knowledge/ counselling and medical advice to general public regarding the vices of the trust like smack, smoking, use of alcoho etc. through organising camps and also to organize the lecture by expert team of doctors, journalists, lawyers, subject specialists, etc.
  3. Promote safe and healthy livins among children and communities.
  4. Provide nutritional support to children of primary stage in drought affected areas.
  5. Work for resource less and poor people by active involvement in rural development through public participation and to coordinate with social activites.
  6. Make every possible efforts to control/manage the situations arising out of natural calamities and to provide relief to victims by securing financial and material support from other institutions , agencies, and persons.
  7. to do work for clean india by way of swach bharat abhiyan and many more.
